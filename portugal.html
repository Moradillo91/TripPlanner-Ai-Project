Portugal

Portugal was a true highlight of our trip. From the soulful streets of Lisbon to the scenic views of Porto, every moment was filled with warmth, tradition, and unforgettable tastes

We’re sharing the routes we followed, the places we loved, and the hidden gems we discovered. Whether you're dreaming of tram rides through Lisbon or wine by the Douro River in Porto, we hope our itineraries help you plan a smoother, more magical trip.
